"In 2028, after the second world plague, two major events happened. Can anyone guess what those two things were?" Mr. Saldon, a Historian, begrudgingly scanned his second grade classroom looking for a student to reveal the answer. Every hand raised as he knew they would. Each with its own overly polite smile. "You." He pointed to the little girl with short fluffy plats. The girl rose from her seat with much poise. A scoff escaped before he could stop it and he busied himself with the chalk resting beneath the board to hide his disdain. He was a man whose opinions were once highly sought after, now sentenced to educate the young minds of a new growing nation.
